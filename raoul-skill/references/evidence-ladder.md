# Evidence Ladder

Source grading for Raoul Pal-style global macro research.

---

## The GMI Disclaimer

Before applying this evidence ladder, understand the confidence constraint:

> **⚠️ Raoul Pal's primary research is published via GMI (Global Macro Investor), a paid institutional service. This skill models his publicly stated methodology only. Proprietary GLI charts, specific model outputs, and trade recommendations from GMI are NOT available in this tool.**

This means:
- GLI phase judgments are based on public central bank data approximations, not Raoul's proprietary models.
- Specific price targets and timing signals attributed to Raoul require GMI access to verify.
- Label outputs as "framework interpretation" when deriving from his public logic applied to public data.

## Confidence calibration by claim type

| Claim type | Confidence level | Required evidence |
|---|---|---|
| GLI direction (public approximation) | Medium | Tier 1: Fed + ECB + BOJ + PBOC balance sheet trends |
| Global M2 in USD terms | Medium | Tier 1: FRED + ECB + PBOC M2 data |
| Credit impulse direction | Medium | Tier 1: BIS credit data, national banking statistics |
| Demographic/debt supercycle position | High | Tier 1: BIS, World Bank, OECD (well-documented) |
| GLI exact level / proprietary chart | Not available | Requires GMI access — do not estimate |
| Specific price targets | Not available | Requires GMI models — do not fabricate |
| Narrative window identification | Medium | Tier 3: correlation analysis + calendar |
| BTC as GLI beta (structural claim) | Medium-High | Tier 2: Raoul's public explanations + historical data |

## When to use the GMI disclaimer

Use this label in output when:
- Translating Raoul's logic to a current market situation using public data.
- Making cycle phase assessments without access to the proprietary GLI model.
- Discussing specific trade structures or price targets associated with Raoul.
