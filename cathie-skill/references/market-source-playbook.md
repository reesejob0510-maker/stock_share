# Market Source Playbook

Use this file when market-specific evidence matters for S-curve and cost curve research.

---

## Global / Multi-Market

**Cost curve benchmarks**
- BloombergNEF (BNEF): Battery Price Survey (annual), EV Outlook, New Energy Outlook.
- IRENA: Renewable Power Generation Costs (annual), World Energy Transitions Outlook.
- IEA: Global EV Outlook, World Energy Outlook.
- IFR: World Robotics Report (annual unit shipments and ASP).
- NIH / NHGRI: DNA sequencing cost data (updated regularly at genome.gov).
- Our World in Data: technology cost curves (solar, sequencing, computing) — visual and downloadable.

**Technology adoption data**
- IEA EV data explorer: EV sales by country and model.
- IRENA statistics: solar and wind capacity by country.
- Waymo / Tesla / Mobileye investor presentations: AV miles driven, safety statistics.
- FDA databases: 510(k), PMA, BLA approvals for genomics devices.

**ARK Invest public research**
- ARK Big Ideas (annual): framework and TAM scenarios. Available at ark-invest.com.
- ARK Innovation ETF (ARKK) 13-F filings: quarterly holdings update.
- ARK CEO interviews: Cathie Wood's public commentary on platform convergence.

---

## US Markets

**Company-level filings**
- SEC EDGAR: 10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, DEF 14A.
- Earnings call transcripts: Seeking Alpha, company IR pages.
- Patent search: USPTO (patents.google.com for better search UI).

**Innovation-specific US sources**
- NHTSA AV data: autonomous vehicle crash reports, exemption filings.
- CARB ZEV credits: California Air Resources Board EV adoption tracking.
- DOE Vehicle Technologies Office: EV cost and technology data.
- FDA 510(k) / PMA database: medical device and diagnostic approvals.
- ClinicalTrials.gov: genomic therapy and diagnostic trials.
- DOE National Labs reports: battery, solar, hydrogen cost data.
- DARPA program announcements: early signals on emerging technology investment.

## A-share (China Mainland)

**Innovation policy tracking**
- 国家战略性新兴产业发展规划: government industrial policy defining supported sectors.
- 十四五 (14th Five-Year Plan) sectoral targets: EV penetration, solar capacity, robot density.
- 工信部 (MIIT) announcements: NEV subsidy schedule, battery standard updates, robot policy.
- 发改委 (NDRC) approvals: energy storage project approvals, grid connection policy.
- 碳中和政策 (Carbon Neutrality): 2030/2060 targets and provincial implementation.

**EV and energy adoption data**
- 乘联会 (CPCA): monthly NEV retail sales data (most reliable source for China EV penetration).
- CAAM (中国汽车工业协会): wholesale data.
- MIIT 新能源汽车推广应用推荐车型目录: approved EV model list (subsidy eligibility).
- 国家电网/南方电网: charging infrastructure rollout data.

**Company-level filings**
- 巨潮资讯 (cninfo.com.cn): all A-share filings.
- 互动易 / 上证 e 互动: management Q&A — useful for tracking R&D and cost roadmap.
- 招投标公告: government procurement for EV, solar, medical devices — confirms real orders.

**Key A-share innovation sectors and sources**
| Sector | Key data source |
|---|---|
| NEV / EV batteries | 乘联会, MIIT, CATL / BYD IR |
| Solar / wind energy | IRENA, NEA (国家能源局), CNESA |
| Industrial robotics | IFR, 中国机器人产业联盟 |
| Semiconductor / AI chips | SEMI, CSIA, company 年报 |
| Genomics / biotech | CFDA/NMPA approvals, CDE database |

## Global Clean Energy

**Primary sources**
- BloombergNEF: the most comprehensive cost and adoption database for energy storage, solar, wind.
- IRENA: International Renewable Energy Agency — capacity and cost data by country.
- IEA: policy and outlook reports.
- Wood Mackenzie: energy storage and EV market research (subscription, but public summaries available).
- S&P Global Commodity Insights: battery materials pricing (lithium, cobalt, nickel).

**Materials constraint tracking**
- LME (London Metal Exchange): lithium, cobalt, nickel spot prices.
- Fastmarkets: battery grade lithium carbonate/hydroxide prices.
- USGS Mineral Resources Program: reserves data for critical battery materials.

---

## Genomics / Biotech

**Primary sources**
- FDA databases: 510(k) clearances, PMA approvals, BLA approvals.
- ClinicalTrials.gov: ongoing trials for gene therapy, mRNA, liquid biopsy.
- NIH / NHGRI: genome sequencing cost data, funding for genomics research.
- Pubmed / bioRxiv: academic publications — use for technology validation, not investment timing.
- Company IR (Illumina, Pacific Biosciences, 10x Genomics): sequencer cost and volume data.

**A-share biotech**
- NMPA (国家药品监督管理局) approval database.
- CDE (药品审评中心) approval calendar.
- 中国临床试验注册中心 (ChiCTR): China clinical trial registry.
