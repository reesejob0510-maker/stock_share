# Buffett.skill Prompt Pack

Use these prompts when you want to start quickly.

## Single company — full moat analysis

```text
用 buffett-skill 深度分析 [公司/股票代码]。
联网查年报、财报、历史 ROE、FCF 转化率和管理层资本配置记录，
判断护城河来源和宽度，估算内在价值区间，
并说明安全边际、排序理由和主要风险。
```

```text
Use buffett-skill to deeply analyze [company / ticker].
Check annual reports, earnings transcripts, 5-year ROE trend, FCF conversion, and capital allocation history.
Assess moat source and width, estimate intrinsic value range,
and state the margin of safety, ranking rationale, and main failure conditions.
```

## Sector moat scan

```text
用 buffett-skill 扫描 A 股 [消费/金融/医疗/工业] 板块。
找出护城河最宽、ROE 最稳定、估值在内在价值以下的 5 家公司，
说明每家的护城河来源、财务质量、估值和降级条件。
```

```text
Use buffett-skill to scan US [consumer staples / financials / healthcare / industrials].
Find the 5 companies with the widest moat, most consistent ROE, and best margin of safety.
Explain moat source, financial quality, valuation, and downgrade condition for each.
```

## Challenge a popular stock

```text
用 buffett-skill 挑战 [公司]。
它真的有护城河吗？护城河来源是什么，证据够不够？
毛利率稳定吗，FCF 和净利润匹配吗？
以现在的价格，市场在假设什么增长？什么情况说明这个判断错了？
```

```text
Use buffett-skill to challenge [company].
Does it actually have a moat? What is the source, and is the evidence strong?
Is gross margin stable? Does FCF match reported earnings?
At the current price, what growth is already priced in? What would make the thesis wrong?
```

## Compare candidates

```text
用 buffett-skill 比较 [A]、[B]、[C]。
按护城河宽度、财务质量（ROE/FCF）、管理层资本配置、估值安全边际、主要风险排序，
并说明哪家排最前面，原因是什么。
```

```text
Use buffett-skill to compare [A], [B], and [C].
Rank by moat width, financial quality (ROE / FCF), capital allocation track record,
valuation margin of safety, and main risk. Explain why the top-ranked company earns that position.
```

## Valuation check only

```text
用 buffett-skill 给 [公司] 估值。
计算正常化所有者收益，用 10% 折现率做 DCF，
再和历史估值区间对比，给出内在价值区间和安全边际。
假设我知道这家公司有宽护城河，重点是价格合不合理。
```

```text
Use buffett-skill to value [company].
Calculate normalized owner earnings, apply DCF at 10% discount rate,
and cross-check against historical trading multiples.
Assume I already believe in the moat — focus on whether the price offers a margin of safety.
```

## Research partner / learning mode

```text
用 buffett-skill 陪我分析 [公司]。
不要直接写报告，每轮先给判断，再问我一个最重要的问题，
带我从商业模式拆到护城河、财务质量和估值。
```

```text
Use buffett-skill as a research partner on [company].
Don't write a report. Each turn: give your judgment first, then ask me one focused question.
Walk me from the business model through moat identification, financial quality, and valuation.
```

## Scorecard mode

```text
用 buffett-skill 的护城河打分表给 [公司] 打分。
每个维度用强/中/弱标注证据，并说明是否值得优先研究。
```

```text
Use buffett-skill's moat scorecard to score [company].
Rate every dimension as strong, medium, weak, or needs checking.
State whether the company clears the bar for further research.
```
