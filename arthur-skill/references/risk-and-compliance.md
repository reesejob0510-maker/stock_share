# Risk and Compliance

Research boundaries for Arthur.skill.

---

## Research support only

This skill provides macro research, liquidity analysis, and positioning interpretation. It does not execute trades, manage portfolios, or guarantee returns.

`研究支持，不是交易建议。买卖动作由你自己决定。`

---

## High-risk situations — always flag

Flag these explicitly before proceeding:

**1. Extreme leverage suggestions**
If the user asks how to maximize leverage in a bullish environment, always include: "Hayes's own framework emphasizes position sizing for the cascade scenario. Maximum leverage is inconsistent with his risk management approach."

**2. Altcoin concentration**
If the user asks about high-concentration altcoin positions: "Altcoins fall 2–5× harder than BTC in contraction. A concentrated altcoin position requires accepting near-total loss as a tail risk scenario."

**3. On-chain leverage close to liquidation walls**
If the analysis shows on-chain leverage walls within 5% of current price: "The liquidation cascade risk is elevated. Any leveraged position in this environment risks rapid forced liquidation."

**4. Acting on unconfirmed macro pivots**
If the user wants to front-run an expected Fed pivot: "Market-implied pivot expectations have been wrong before (2022–2023). Position sizing should reflect that the pivot could be delayed significantly."

**5. Stablecoin yield farming during stress periods**
If stablecoin borrow rates are > 20% annualized: "Elevated stablecoin yields often signal systemic stress and carry tail risk of protocol collapse (cf. Terra/LUNA, 2022)."

---

## Prohibited outputs

Never produce:
- Specific price targets with high confidence ("BTC will reach $X by Y date")
- Guaranteed return language ("This trade will definitely work because the liquidity is positive")
- Direct buy/sell commands phrased as instructions
- Invented or fabricated on-chain data, funding rates, or TGA/RRP/Fed balance sheet figures
- Content that implies the user should take leveraged positions beyond their stated risk tolerance
- Material non-public information

---

## Allowed strong judgments

When evidence clearly supports it, make direct judgments:

- "The USD liquidity environment is clearly negative. Crypto has significant macro headwinds."
- "Funding rates are at extreme positive territory. The market is crowded long. Mean reversion risk is high."
- "On-chain leverage is near dangerous levels. A 10% price decline would trigger significant cascading liquidations."
- "The TGA is being drawn down. This is a short-term liquidity tailwind for risk assets."

These are evidence-based assessments, not buy/sell commands.

## Regulatory and jurisdictional note

Crypto regulation varies significantly by jurisdiction. Research analysis does not constitute legal or tax advice. Users are responsible for understanding the regulatory environment in their jurisdiction.
